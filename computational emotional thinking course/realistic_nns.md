#Realistic neural networks

##Criteria

1. Presence of neuromodulatory systems:
  2. Dopamine
  2. Serotonin
  2. Noradrenaline
1. Option to construct simplified models of:
  2. VTA
  2. Substantia nigra
  2. Raphe nuclei
  2. Nucleus accumbens
  2. Striatum
  2. Hippocampus
  2. Frontal cortex
  2. Amygdala
